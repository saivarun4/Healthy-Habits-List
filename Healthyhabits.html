Healthy Habits

  1. Daily Get up early
  2. Do some exercise
  3. Eat healthy breakfast without fail
  4. Start your work as early as possible
  5. Do not sleep or take nap during morning hours
  6. After working for sometime take litle breaks in between

    • Start taking breaks after working for few hours
    • Take breaks of not more than 15 minutes
    • Do not take more breaks in a day

  7. Evening spend some time as "ME" time
  8. Eat 2 hours before going to bed for healthy digestion
  9. Go to bed early
